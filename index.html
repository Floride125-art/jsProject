AKAN NAMES

Welcome to this page for calculating your Akan Name

About

This is web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abena
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Plz fill the form below and get your Akan Name!
